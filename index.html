About Me

My name is Kristy Yeaton. I live in Portland Maine and work as a web developer. I strongly believe in well written, clean and well documented code. I also enjoy bringing complex designs to life and making the user experience smooth and fast. My ideal job is working closely with a design team to make new and innovative UI components, tools and best practices to share with the community.

Though I do not consider myself a designer I do enjoy doing small design tasks such as making a mobile design based on a fully flushed out desktop only design or adding special effects such as hovers or animations to a static design during implementation. I also enjoy helping educate designers on new front end technologies and help them adapt them into their work.
